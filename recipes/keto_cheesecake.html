Keto Cheesecake

Cheesecake for people on Keto diets

Although this is a Keto cheesecake it's almost indistinguishable from regular cheesecake.

Perhaps the only difference is the crust. Oh, and be warned, there is a laxative effect with certain sweeteners.

To avoid severe gastrointestinal consequences I suggest using monkfruit instead of erythritol or xylitol.

Before you get started you will need a 10-inch spingform pan to bake this in.

Crust Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of almond flour
  • 1/4 cup of monkfruit sweetener
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • 6 tbsp of melted butter

Filling Ingredients

  • 48 ounces of cream cheese
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 2 cups of monkfruit sweetener
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla extract
  • 5 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 Fahrenheit
  2. Mix the crust ingredients, adding the butter last
  3. Form the crust around the springform pan and set it in the fridge to set up for 15 minutes.
  4. Add the filling ingredients one at a time (preferably into a stand mixer) and mix.
  5. Add the filling to the crust and put it in the oven for 45 minutes.
  6. At this point you should start checking the consistency for doneness (the top should look matte).
  7. Let the cake cool in the oven for at least half an hour or it might split.
  8. Remove the cake and loosen it with a knife then let it cool until you're ready to serve it.

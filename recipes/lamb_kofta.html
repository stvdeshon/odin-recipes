Lamb Kofta

Ground Lamb with warm spices

This is a recipe for ground lamb that you might have had if you've ever been to a Greek or Middle Eastern restaurant.

This dish can be cooked on a grill, over charcoal, or on a pan. The choice is yours.

It can be served on a stick or right on a plate.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground lamb
  • 1 tsp of minced garlic
  • 1/2 a grated yellow onion
  • 2 tsp of coriander
  • 1 tsp of cumin powder
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp of allspice
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • 1/2 tsp of ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp of fresh finely chopped parsley
  • 1/2 tsp of black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix the spices together and then mix that into the lamb.
  2. Roll the lamb into little logs and cook them until done.
